##Introduction

Grammatical type: adjective.   
Occurrences:   7x HB (7/0/0); 0x Sir; 0x Qum; 0x Inscr.  (Total: 7x).

* Torah: Gen 30:35, 39, 40; 31:8 (2x), 10, 12.

## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> <span dir="rtl">עקד</span> distinguishes goats in the flock by ‘patches’ or ‘markings’, most likely on 
the legs or feet. These patches were probably ‘white’ for goats and ‘dark brown’ or ‘black’ for sheep. 
<span dir="rtl">ׁעקד</span> predominately serves as a descriptor of goats.
