### Text Doubtful

<b>A.1</b> 
The Sira scroll from Masada displays the following line, in which the word <span dir="rtl">שִׁפְחָה</span> is partially reconstructed:
<span dir="rtl">מהתעשק עׄ[ם שפ]חׄהׄ לך</span>,
‘be ashamed of exploiting your [serv]ant-girl’.
However, the restoration is disputed.
