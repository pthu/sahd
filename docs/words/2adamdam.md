##Introduction

Grammatical type: adjective.   
Occurrences:   6x HB (6/0/0); 0x Sir; 0x Qum; 0x Inscr.  (Total: 6x).

* Torah: Lev 13:19, 24, 42, 43, 49; 14:37.


## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> <span dir="rtl">אדמדם</span> conveys ‘bright red’. In the Heb. Scriptures it is used to describe the 
colour of a skin infection as an infected burn as well as a mould, a fungus that grows in material made 
out of leather, wool, or linen and a fungus growing in the walls of a house.
