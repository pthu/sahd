* Torah: Gen 49:12.


## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> In the light of the Akk. cognate <i>ekēlu</i>, <span dir="rtl">חכלילי</span> conveys ‘a dark or a dark 
red’ colour. In its only extant occurrence it describes the dark colour of a person’s eyes resulting from 
the liberal consumption of wine. This lexeme appears to have a restrictive syntagmatic usage.
