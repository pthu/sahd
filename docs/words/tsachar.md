##Introduction

Grammatical type:  noun masculine.  
Occurrences:   1x HB (0/1/0); 0x Sir; 0x Qum; 0x Inscr.  (Total: 1x).

* Nebiim: Ezek 27:18.

## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> The parallelism in Ezek 27:18 favours identifying <span dir="rtl">צחר</span> as a toponym. If <span 
dir="rtl">צחר</span> is a colour lexeme, Syr. and Arab. cognates suggest that it stands for ‘a light, 
shiny colour’ with a tint of either yellow or red. The focus of this lexeme is on brilliance.
