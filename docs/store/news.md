## News

### Takamitsu Muraoka 1938-2026

We received the sad news that on February 10, 2026, professor Takamitsu Muraoka passed away at the age of 88 years. Professor Muraoka was chair of the SAHD project from 1995 to 2008. He was also the editor of two 
<a href="../printed_publications/">printed SAHD publications</a>.
An 
<a href=“../../pdfs/IM_Muraoka.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">In Memoriam</a>
was written by his former student Martin van Baasten.
