Occurrences:   0x HB; 0x Sir; 0x Qum; 1x Inscr.  (Total: 1x)

* Inscr.: Hebron jar inscription.


## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> If <span dir="rtl">כחל</span> represents a colour, it is most likely ‘dark, dark blue’. Otherwise <span 
dir="rtl">כחל</span> in this inscription is a toponym.

<b>For this entry, see further John E. Hartley, <i>The Semantics of Ancient Hebrew Colour Lexemes</i> (2010), 211-213 (<a href="/store/printed_publications/">Printed publications</a>).</b>
