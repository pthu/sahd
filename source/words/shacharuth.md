## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> 
The precise meaning of <span dir="rtl">שׁחרות</span> has not been settled. In its present setting 
<span dir="rtl">שׁחרות</span> intensifies the significance of the preceding lexeme <span 
dir="rtl">ילדות</span>, ‘youth’, to which it is syndetically joined. The most viable explanation to date 
is that <span dir="rtl">שׁחרות</span> comes from the Heb. root <span dir="rtl">שׁחר</span> ‘black’ and stands for a youth’s 
glowing black hair.

<b>For this entry, see further John E. Hartley, <i>The Semantics of Ancient Hebrew Colour Lexemes</i> (2010), 71-73 (<a href="/store/printed_publications/">Printed publications</a>).</b>
