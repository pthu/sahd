## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> <span dir="rtl">טלא</span> identifies members of a flock, particularly goats, having patches or wide 
streaks of varying colours. It, thus, references a pattern of colours rather than a specific hue (Brenner 
1982:169). <span dir="rtl">טלא</span> also serves to describe resplendent curtains or quilts artfully 
made out of multicoloured fabrics, and it is used as a descriptor for patched sandals.

<b>For this entry, see further John E. Hartley, <i>The Semantics of Ancient Hebrew Colour Lexemes</i> (2010), 156-159 (<a href="/store/printed_publications/">Printed publications</a>).</b>
