## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> <span dir="rtl">כחל</span> describes painting the area around the eyes, especially the edges of the 
eyelids, with a ‘dark or dark bluish’ powder. In Early BHeb. <span dir="rtl">כחל</span> was a highly 
referential lexeme. The adjectival form <span dir="rtl">כחול</span> serves as the word for ‘blue’ in 
modern Heb.

<b>For this entry, see further John E. Hartley, <i>The Semantics of Ancient Hebrew Colour Lexemes</i> (2010), 210-211 (<a href="/store/printed_publications/">Printed publications</a>).</b>
