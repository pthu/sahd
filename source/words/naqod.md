* Torah: Gen 30:32 (2x), 33, 35, 39; 31:8 (2x), 10, 12.


## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> The colour lexeme <span dir="rtl">נקד</span> occurs only in Gen always in referencing ‘small spots’ 
on a animal’s coat. Most likely the colour of the spots was a non-dominant colour for the given 
species; for goats the spots were white and black for sheep. <span dir="rtl">נקד</span>, then, was a 
contextualised colour lexeme used by ancient shepherds for identifying members of the flock with 
small dots or specks.

<b>For this entry, see further John E. Hartley, <i>The Semantics of Ancient Hebrew Colour Lexemes</i> (2010), 145-149 (<a href="/store/printed_publications/">Printed publications</a>).</b>
