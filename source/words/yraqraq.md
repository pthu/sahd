## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> The pattern of reduplication in <span dir="rtl">ירקרק</span> conveys a ‘brilliant green or yellow’. It 
describes the colour of mould or fungus that sometimes grows on a garment or in the walls of a 
house and the ‘shiny, glistening golden’ colour of a dove’s wings illuminated by the sun (Ps 68:14).
