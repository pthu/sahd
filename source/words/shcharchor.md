##Introduction

* Ketubim: Cant 1:6.

## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> 
<span dir="rtl">שׁחרחר</span> represents a colour range from ‘very dark brown’ to ‘rich black’, the 
reduplication conveys a deeper dark colour.

<b>For this entry, see further John E. Hartley, <i>The Semantics of Ancient Hebrew Colour Lexemes</i> (2010), 79-80 (<a href="/store/printed_publications/">Printed publications</a>).</b>
