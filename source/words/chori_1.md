##Introduction

* Torah: Gen 40:16.

## 7. Conclusion

<b>A.1</b> <span dir="rtl">חרי</span> denotes ‘white’. In its sole occurrence in Heb. Scriptures, it functions as a 
subst. for ‘white bread or pastry’.

<b>For this entry, see further John E. Hartley, <i>The Semantics of Ancient Hebrew Colour Lexemes</i> (2010), 89-91 (<a href="/store/printed_publications/">Printed publications</a>).</b>
